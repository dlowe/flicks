How this works

We do the aggregating, you do the filtering — together we make a curated calendar of just the stuff you're interested in.

Every upcoming screening from Portland's indie & repertory theaters lands here, with wide releases and non-film events already stripped out. Then you tune it:

  • Hit × to hide a film, a theater, or a whole weekday — what's left is your calendar. ⌘Z undoes anything, so nothing's permanent.
  • A green New tag flags showings you haven't seen yet.
  • Hidden a film? When it gets a fresh screening it slips back in — faded, with a + to keep it — so "not right now" never means "never again."
  • Switch by date / film / theater, and Add to calendar exports whatever's left.
  • Built a set you like on your phone? copy link (in the bar at the bottom) carries your whole filter set to another device — just paste it there.

It all saves in this browser — walk away, come back, reload, keep filtering. Your curated calendar builds up over time; there's nothing to set up or sign into.

All showtimes are Pacific time.
